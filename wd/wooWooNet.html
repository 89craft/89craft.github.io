Woo Woo Network

The objective of this sponsored capstone project was to create a website where Healers can post their services and accept payments from clients and for clients to book appointments with healers.

The features my team completed include:

  • Installing a image/video loader,

  • Uploading of images/videos to website,

  • Adding images/videos to reviews and profiles,

  • Installing of a payment system,

  • Making payments to other users,

  • Managing payment accounts via UI,

  • Accessing the email address of another user for communication,

  • Booking an appointment,

  • Managing an appointment schedule, and

  • Adding tags to reviews,

My tasks for this project included:

  • Design a new logo,

  • Create logo as an SVG,

  • Design a color scheme for the logo and website,

  • Design all website pages (mobile and desktop),

  • Protoype website (mobile and desktop),

  • Create website pages with React, and

  • Program website pages,
